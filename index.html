Usage Guide

1. Switch Context

Use the toggle in the header to switch between Christmas and Black Friday keywords, as well as US and DE markets.

2. Select Keywords

Click keywords to add them to your cart. Click again to remove.

3. Copying Data

  • Copy List: Copies all keywords visible in the current category view.
  • Copy Selection: Copies specific keywords you have selected manually.

4. German Mode (DE)

In DE mode, keywords show the German term (Bold) and English translation (Gray). Copy actions will only copy the German term.
